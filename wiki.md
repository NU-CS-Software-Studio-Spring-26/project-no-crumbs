# No Crumbs wiki

## What Problem Does This App Solve?

Coordinating shared meals with friends is surprisingly hard.  This means that there are many missed opportunities for these connections with friends.  **No Crumbs** solves this by giving friends a dedicated space to post about meals they're cooking or leftovers they want to share.  This makes it much easier for friends to connect over food.

## Speculative Features 

These are potential features beyond our MVP (list subject to change)

- **Notifications** — In-app/email alerts when someone responds to your post
- **Dietary tags** — Tag posts with certain dietary info
- **Location filters** — Filter posts by proximity
- **Photo uploads** — Attach a photo of the meal to a post
- **Ratings** — Leave a short review after a shared meal
- **Mobile-responsive design** — Optimized layout for phones
